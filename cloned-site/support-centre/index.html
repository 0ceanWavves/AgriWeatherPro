OpenWeather’s support centre is not only a team of specialists ready to help you with any issue but also a set of tools and resources designed to make it easier for you to use our services. Whether you are just starting out with APIs, an experienced developer, or a business, our team is ready to provide answers to your questions.

OpenWeather AI assistant Ulla

Ulla, the OpenWeather AI assistant, is an AI-powered tool trained on our products and services to provide seamless support. Whether you are exploring APIs, troubleshooting issues, or seeking guidance, Ulla is designed to aid you in navigating OpenWeather's product offerings.

What is Ulla: it is the advanced AI technology powering the OpenWeather assistant. Originally developed as a corporate AI assistant, Ulla specialises in transcriptions, summaries, and secure data management, supporting business across industries with innovative solutions. Explore more at ulla.bot.
Ulla is just starting out in beta and may make some mistakes. Consider checking important information. We are working to improve and develop the assistant to give the most accurate answers. Let us know if you notice any mistakes, and we will correct it.

FAQ
FAQ is a collection with a detailed information about most popular topics

Payment process

The "Payment process" chapter is designed to help customers navigate various aspects of the payment process more effectively and reduce the number of inquiries related to payment issues. In this section, you'll find guidance on:
- Changing Your Tariff Plan: Instructions on how to effortlessly modify your current plan.
- Obtaining an Invoice: Steps to request and receive your invoices quickly and easily.
- Adjusting Payment Information: Information on how to update your payment methods and invoice details as needed.
- Accepted Payment Methods: A comprehensive list of the payment options available for your convenience.
- Payment Processing including Information about security measures in place to protect payment information.
- Troubleshooting Payment Issues: Helpful tips for resolving common payment problems you may encounter.

Personal account

The "Personal Account" chapter aims to assist users in understanding and managing their personal accounts effectively. This chapter covers topics related to account management, and troubleshooting, ensuring users can quickly find the information they need. Key areas include:
- Account Management: Guidance on updating personal details such as email address and password, as well as how to set preferences and notification settings.
- Subscription Limits Exceeded: Information on what to do if you subscription limits are surpassed.
- API Call Count: Instructions on how to locate information regarding your API calls and guidance on calculating them.

Subscriptions and collection

The Professional API Collection section is designed to assist in navigating in the the available subscriptions and the products included with each plan. This section covers the following key topics:
- Types of Products and Data Included: Comprehensive details on the various APIs available.
- How to Choose the Right Subscription: Evaluate the different subscription plans and select the one that best meets your specific needs and objectives.
- Subscription Payments: Access detailed information about the various payment methods available.

API errors

The API Errors chapter is go-to guide for understanding the various error codes you might encounter while using our API. By familiarizing with most common errors, you’ll be better equipped to diagnose problems and troubleshoot effectively. For each error code, we provide a clear explanation of what it means and how it relates to your request or operation and describe common reasons why errors might occur, such as incorrect parameters, authentication failures, or connectivity issues. This information is designed to help you identify the issue quickly and take appropriate action.
